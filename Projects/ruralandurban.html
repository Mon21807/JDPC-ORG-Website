Our Mission

At JDPC, we believe in building communities, not just houses. Through our Rural and Urban Development initiatives, we bring essential services like clean water, hygiene promotion, and shelter to vulnerable families in restoring dignity and transforming lives, one project at a time.

WASH, NFIs & Female Hygiene Kits

Access to clean water and proper sanitation is a basic human right. In Bokkos communities, we've reached hundreds of families with WASH facilities, distributed life-saving Non-Food Items (NFIs), and Female Hygiene Kits that address critical health and hygiene needs.

Funded by: Mission 21 & CRS

Rehabilitation of Houses – Wayar Mangor

Natural disasters and conflict have displaced many in Bokkos. Our house rehabilitation program in Wayar Mangor focuses on rebuilding homes with durable structures and restoring safe living conditions. Every nail and every brick tells a story of hope, resilience, and community rebirth.

Funded by: CRS

Eco-friendly Agriculture

At JDPC, we focus on educating communities about advanced Eco-friendly Agriculture techniques—offering innovative solutions to food sustainability and improving the use of traditional farming practices.

Funded by: LDSC & CRS

CRS Partnership

Our collaboration with Catholic Relief Services (CRS) focuses on providing clean water, sanitation, and agricultural support to vulnerable families in Bokkos, enhancing community resilience and health.

Key Initiatives:

  • Installation of WASH facilities
  • Distribution of hygiene kits
  • Eco-friendly Agriculture training
  • Community Workshops

Impact: Over 1,000 families gained access to clean water and 300 farmers adopted eco-friendly agriculture, improving livelihoods in communities.

Training Program

JDPC's Training Program empowers Bokkos communities with two key initiatives: WASH Training for clean water and hygiene, and Eco-friendly Agriculture Training for sustainable farming.

WASH Training:

  • Water purification techniques
  • Water facility maintenance
  • Hygiene awareness workshops

Eco-friendly Agriculture Training:

  • Crop rotation and soil management
  • Organic farming techniques
  • High-yield seed distribution

Impact: Over 700 farmers and community members trained, with a 35% increase in crop yields and improved access to clean water for 1,200 households.
